    SFS

    Siemens Financial Services

    EMI-Based Dealer Financing · 6–12 Month Tenure
    How It Works
    • Distributor-focused EMI solutions for 6 or 12 months
    • Dealer pays a one-time upfront facility fee (+ 18% GST) — no recurring interest
    • Fee varies by tenure and dealer's credit terms with Stryker (0 to 90 days)
    • EMI starts 30 days after invoicing
    • Stryker receives full payment within 90 days
    • Applicable to all channel partners with 1+ year relationship

    TC

    Tata Capital

    Extended Credit / Overdraft Facility · Up to 180 Days
    How It Works
    • Works like an OD or CC limit — not traditional EMI
    • Stryker's existing credit period (0–90 days) remains interest-free
    • Tata Capital provides an additional 90 days beyond Stryker's credit period
    • Interest charged per-day for the Tata Capital period only
    • Dealer gets a dedicated credit limit for Stryker purchases
    • Can finance any number of bills within the approved limit
